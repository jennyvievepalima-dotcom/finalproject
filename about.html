IN CHICKEN ATI-ATIHAN
SASAYAW KA SA SARAP!

Cromwell’s Grill Chicken Ati-Atihan is a Filipino restaurant
in Laoag City known for its flavorful grilled chicken and classic Filipino dishes.
Inspired by the lively Ati-Atihan tradition, we serve quality, affordable meals in a warm and welcoming environment.

History of the Chicken Ati-Atihan

The Ati-atihan Festival, which is held every January in the Town of Kalibo, Aklan, in the Island of Panay, which is the wildest among Philippine fiestas. Celebrants paint their faces with black soot and wear bright-colored outlandish costumes as they dance in revelry during the last three days of this week-long festival.

Chicken Ati-atihan started its operations in Kalibo, Aklan and Roxas City, Capiz in 2003.

The restaurant’s very unique recipe and marketing strategy has tailored to appear to, and it suited the Filipino market since it opened its doors.

Ati-atihan is the first native style restaurant in the country that serves chicken barbecue in its own style and own taste. Its mouth-watering promise — a combination of good food and ambiance. Once you taste, sasayaw ka sa sarap because in Chicken Ati-atihan, “We didn’t just invent chicken barbeque, we perfected it.”
